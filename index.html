      Research
    I am broadly interested in large-scale processing systems, high performance computing, storage systems, file systems and databases. As part of my PhD research, I am developing efficient and cost-effective techniques for the Approximate Nearest Neighbour (ANN) problem using GPUs. My Ph.D. advisor is Dr. Jyothi Vedurada
